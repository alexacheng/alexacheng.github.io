    Ori - AR platform design

    “ORI” is an AR glasses product for tourists based on commercial cultural travel scenarios. During the whole developing process, I mainly participated in the concept development stage, ideation, model making, prototyping and rendering output.

    The previous version of product was a bit heavy, hard to manipulate (adjust the band length etc.), complex structure. After some consideration we decided to make a “lighter” version, which only includes a optical module, a main chip and two amplifiers.
