    Weed - A portable lamp design

    Weed is an easy-charging lighting product which aims at “providing users with agile and convenient lighting”. At the same time, by using the traditional Chinese imagery it arises people’s emotions towards nature and connects this feeling together with people’s daily lives.

    My inspiration comes from early childhood. The dogtail grass (毛毛草) is the most memorial part in Chinese people’s early lives. During when children often ran outdoor with it in their hands. My grandma often weaved a grass rabbit for me to play with when I was young. This kind of toy is easy to make, low cost, while also intimate to people. It symbolizes a meaning of kindness, freedom and wildness. Making handcrafts from wild grass can also be viewed as a way of communication.

    There is a large open space in our community, which used to be a par- adise for children to go out and play after dinner ten years ago when I was young. Now this place has been stained red by red lights. No longer any child in sight.

    By doing interviews and online research, I discovered some fundemental reasons of kids not going outdoor. One of the most frequently-mentioned reasons is “Playing video games”.

    During the design process what kept making me feel confused was the lighting’s base design. I want it to have the light and free feeling of plants, it can grow everywhere, so it has to be easy to hold, easy to put it on any surfaces. After comparing the straight form and the pointy form, I chose the pointy form to offer it with the natural feeling of plant growth and bifurcation. Then is the technical part. It hard to fit the inner structure like coils and chips into the nutshell, for the size of the coil has long diameter. After try different forms of both shell and stem structure I finally combine these together.
