    Side Pocket #

    The online lab reservation system in our school was really poor designed and annoying. The students who want to use lab have to reserve online from an online platform, while they’ve complained many times for its poor work flow structure design and invisible reviewing status.

    In order to slove this problem, I designed the app Side Pocket. It is an online platform made for booking laboratories, equipements online. It can improve and simplify the rental process to achieve more efficient communication between user and the administrator, students and teachers.
