    Land Scar #

    “Rethink about the bond between youself and nature.”

    “ Landscar ” is a conceptual art installation project proposal, aiming at evoking people’s reflection on the relationship between nature and themselves.

    Idea came during one of my outings, when I found many wild mushrooms growing from tree trunks and from ground. Why are they there? What do they mean to me – these mushrooms and bug bite holes? Sometimes they are too common for us to notice, yet it doesn’t mean they are nothing to human.

    Installed on larged room corners and walls, the installation will “breathe” continously according to the overall people density and distance between installation. When the distance/density raised, the “cell” will breathe more frequently. Otherwise the frequency will slow down.
