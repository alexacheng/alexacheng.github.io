    Breezy - A pneumatic experiment

    Breezy is a product designed to help people with scars to care for their neck health and ear health.

    “Scar” is always associated closly with illness, tor- ture, shame, and the past. And it will gradually become a symbolic long-term existence as time goes by, which also bring people with sad and unbearable memories. People, es- pecially young women who are left with scars often become unconfident and low self-esteem.

    After research both from online and offline, I discovered that people who have scars always try to use thick and wide jewlery to cover their scars under cloths, necklac- es or bracelets. Some people also choose tatoo on their scars.

    However, inproper ways may cause serious health is- sues, like inflammation and permanent skin problems.

    “ Scar is your breathing beauty.”

    Breezy is a health data monitor which can be pasted on the scar through a skin-friendly medical silicone patch. It al- lows users to get a visual feed- back by simulating cellular res- piration in nature. The breathing frequency is highly influenced by your personal health condition. With the more severe problem of young people’s hearing loss and cervical spondylosis, Breezy aims to provide real-time monitoring your ear & neck health, by small sensors embedded inside, and then transmit data to your phone in time. The visual output is been visualized by cell “breathing”.
