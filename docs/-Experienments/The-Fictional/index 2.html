    The Fictional #

    Some fiction sketches I did, trying to seek for the promising future together with the possible co-existence of fine art, advanced technology, visible morality and architecture. What could be human’s life be like in the next 500 years?

    We are all familiar with Jules Gabriel Verne ’s wonderful science fiction novels, we often regard these as “impossible” scenarios. But these novels also provide people with a bunch of possibilities of living under different conditions, fighting with their own weakness and in pursuit for self-evolution.
